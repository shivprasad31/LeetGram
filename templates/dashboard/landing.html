Built for the full learning loop

More than a problem tracker. It is a serious student operating system.

The product flow stays clean and minimal, but the underlying system supports social graph features, revision mechanics, async sync jobs, and live updates.

Challenge friends on overlap

Generate fair timed duels from questions both users already solved, then compare speed, score, and result history.

Turn solved into remembered

Revision lists, notes, spaced repetition timing, and reminder queues make old problems useful again instead of forgotten.

Practice in groups

Groups power practice cohorts and local competition so improvement feels social, not isolated.

Why this matters

Students rarely fail from lack of material. They fail from fragmented momentum.

LeetWise brings profile sync, progress visibility, shared competition, and review discipline into a single rhythm. That is the design principle behind the whole platform.
